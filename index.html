About Me

Bobby Schwartz is a professional Graphic Designer and is working towards certification on fullstack web development. Additionally, he has experience as a Production Artist, Graphic Designer, and problem-solver in print, advertising, property development and apparel industries. In addition to production experience, he is also an active fine artist and constantly learning new software, including 3D rendering applications such as Blender. Bobby currently resides in the New Hampshire Seacoast area and is an avid skier, motorcyclist, hiker, and pet owner.
